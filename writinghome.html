My Writing

Here's my writing! Most of it seems to surround my love of good systems and my hate of signals that people confuse themselves for systems. This is not to say I hate signals, just ones that are conflated with the system they're really a proxy for. Signals are tools not truths. If you like this idea, you may enjoy what I have linked here! I do not know anything about analytical philosophy nor do I have any kind of formal arts/culture education, so don't expect anything too crazy ;)
