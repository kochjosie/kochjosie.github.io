Hello, thanks for stopping by! My name is Josie. I love making things, and this is my home on the web where I share those things that I make. You'll find my thoughts on things on my blog. It's a mix of serious and not-so-serious blurbs about my life, my projects, and the world around me. You can find my creative coding sketch gallery in the coding section along with information on creative coding. In the electronics section, you can find more hardware based art and projects. The art section is where I post traditional art and may end up posting music in the future. The 'other makes' section is for just that, other makes. I also enjoy fiber arts and cooking, so that's the bulk of that section. If you're looking for a way to get in touch, the contact section is there too. I hope you enjoy your time here!
