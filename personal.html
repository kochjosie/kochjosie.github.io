About Me & Contact Info.

Hello! My name is Josephine Koch. I like making art, hobby electronics, creative coding, writing, and some other stuff too. I have a strong love for iterative problem solving and exploration, which is an approach I try to bring to everything I do. To quote Dwarf Fortress, "Losing is fun!". I believe life becomes manageable when you treat it like a series of experiments with the goal of failing because failing is learning if you're doing it right. While I do believe in a greater purpose to life, I don't believe this greater purpose is the right way to approach the day. In daily life, you only have means not ends. These are all ideas I want to address through my projects, writing, and artwork, which you can find here.
